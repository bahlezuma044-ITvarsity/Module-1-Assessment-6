Product Description.

AirPods are wireless Bluetooth earbuds designed by Apple. They were first released on September 7, 2016, with a second generation released in March 2019. They are Apple's entry-level wireless headphones, sold alongside the AirPods Pro and AirPods Max. Within two years, they became Apple's most popular accessory.


Product Information.

AirPods Pro have been designed to deliver Active Noise Cancellation for immersive sound, Transparency mode so you can hear your surroundings, and a customizable fit for all-day comfort. Just like AirPods, AirPods Pro connect magically to your iPhone or Apple Watch. And they’re ready to use right out of the case.

Features:

Active Noise Cancellation for immersive sound:

AirPods Pro are the only in-ear headphones with Active Noise Cancellation that continuously adapts to the geometry of your ear and the fit of the ear tips — blocking out the world so you can focus on what you’re listening to.

Transparency mode for hearing what’s happening around you:

Want to hear what’s happening around you? Just press and hold the force sensor on the stem to move between Active Noise Cancellation and Transparency mode which lets outside sound in, and allows things to sound and feel natural when you’re talking to people nearby.

A customizable fit for all-day comfort:

Apple has redefine the details of comfort, creating a new class of in-ear headphones with a customisable fit that forms an exeptional seal for active noise cancellation, you'll feel the music and not your headphones.
