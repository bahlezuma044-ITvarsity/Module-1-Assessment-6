Product Description.

iCare Plus for MacBook Pro extends your warranty coverage to three years from the original date of purchase of you MacBook Pro. iCare Plus also includes an Apple Authorised screen repair, which will retain your MacBook Pro warranty should your screen need to be replaced. Screen damage is limited to one incident within a 36 month period and is subject to a service fee.
